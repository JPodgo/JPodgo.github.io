]]> Remote sensing of lacustrine icebergs ../../../art-san-quintin/index.html Thu, 11 Apr 2024 11:03:27 +0000 ../../../index.html?p=47 Read More]]> In this article I present the methods and results of the work I have been doing with Michał Pętlicki, PhD, in the field of iceberg remote sensing. The article contains a detailed description of a procedure for detection of icebergs on high-resolution satellite imagery and a discussion of the statistical properties of the population of icebergs in the proglacial lake of the San Quintin glacier in southern Chile. This fruit of Polish-Chilean cooperation has been released in mid-2020 and has been cited 8 times since.
